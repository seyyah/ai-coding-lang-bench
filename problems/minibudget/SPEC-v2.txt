MiniBudget Specification
Version: 2.0 (Extends v1.0)

New commands:
    minibudget delete <index>
    minibudget summary

========================================
1. Command Specifications
========================================

1.1 delete <index>
Remove entry at 1-based index from data.txt.
Print: "Deleted index <index>"

1.2 summary
Print total balance (sum of all amounts).
Format: "Total Balance: <sum>"
